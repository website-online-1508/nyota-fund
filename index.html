About the NYOTA Fund

NYOTA Fund is a national financial empowerment initiative focused on supporting youth entrepreneurs across Kenya through accessible and affordable funding opportunities.

Our goal is to stimulate economic growth by supporting small businesses, promoting innovation, and providing capital to individuals ready to grow their economic potential.
